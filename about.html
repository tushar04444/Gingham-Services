About Us

Gingham Services Ltd is a software company based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We started with website design & development, but over time expanded into full-service software and mobile app development. Known as the best web design company in Bangladesh, we are strategists, designers, producers, and technologists who share a passion for creating great ideas and translating them into engaging user experiences and meaningful business relationships.

Our core principles and beliefs:

  • We strongly believe in research and innovation.
  • We focus on delivering high-quality software solutions.
  • We prioritize user experience and interface design.
  • We follow agile and iterative development processes.
  • We provide continuous support and maintenance.
  • We cultivate meaningful business relationships.
